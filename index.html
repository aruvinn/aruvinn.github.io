Hi! 👋

I'm a San Francisco-based UX Designer passionate about creating aesthetically pleasing products without compromising user friendliness.

My days studying at Georgia Institue of Technology in Atlanta, GA, opened my eyes to the field of Human-Computing Interaction. Combined with my love for practical problem-solving and the frustrations that I observed coming from non-native digital citizens when they're trying to learn new programs or apps, it leads me to pursue the field of User Experience.

I believe in lifelong learning, open communications, teamwork, and social responsibility.

When I'm not honing my design/ coding/ ux skills, I channel my energy into pursuing my other passions in sustainable beauty & wellness, finding new boba place in town, or grinding through my ever-growing library of video games.

Current obsession(s): mastering Git, Face Oils, Asha Tea House, and finishing Dark Souls 3.
